## 🔄 Morphing Animation Details

### Border-Radius Morphing
Each blob transitions through 4 unique organic shapes:
```css
0%:   60% 40% 35% 65% / 60% 35% 65% 40%
25%:  35% 65% 65% 35% / 50% 60% 40% 50%
50%:  55% 45% 35% 65% / 35% 65% 65% 35%
75%:  65% 35% 55% 45% / 45% 55% 60% 45%
```

### Transform Animations
- **Translation**: Moves in X/Y directions
- **Scale**: Grows and shrinks (0.88 - 1.18)
- **Rotation**: Full 360° rotation
- **Duration**: 25 seconds per cycle (slower, more elegant)
